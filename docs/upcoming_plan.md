# Owly Editor: Upcoming Feature Roadmap

This document outlines the high-priority features identified for the next phases of development, categorized by business value and implementation complexity.

## Phase 2: Branding & Professional Polish
*Focus: Turning the editor into a business-ready tool.*

### 1. Brand Overlays (Logo/CTA)
- **Status**: ✅ Infrastructure Ready (Track 5 Implemented)
- **Value**: High (Essential for marketing content)
- **Complexity**: Low
- **Description**: Allow users to upload a brand logo and add a "Call to Action" button or text overlay at the end of the video. (Infrastructure delivered via new Overlays track and Image Import).

### 2. AI Auto-Ducking
- **Value**: High (Instant audio quality)
- **Complexity**: Moderate
- **Description**: Automatically reduce background music volume when a voiceover clip is active on the timeline.

### 3. One-Click Cinematic LUTs
- **Value**: Medium
- **Complexity**: Low
- **Description**: Predefined color filters (e.g., "Vibrant," "Moody," "Corporate") to give videos a consistent visual style.

---

## Phase 3: AI Integration & Workflow
*Focus: Deeply integrating the editor with the Owly Studio AI engine.*

### 1. AI Shot Regeneration
- **Value**: Extremely High (Unique Differentiator)
- **Complexity**: High
- **Description**: Add a "Regenerate" button to video clips. Clicking this triggers a new AI generation for that specific scene directly from the timeline.

### 2. Auto-Captions (STT)
- **Value**: Extremely High (Accessibility & Engagement)
- **Complexity**: High
- **Description**: Use speech-to-text to automatically generate subtitle clips with customizable premium styles (e.g., "Alex Hormozi" style captions).

### 3. B-Roll AI Suggester
- **Value**: High
- **Complexity**: High
- **Description**: Analyze voiceover content and suggest/insert relevant stock footage or AI-generated b-roll to enhance visual storytelling.

---

## Phase 4: Scaling & Utility

### 1. Transitions
- **Value**: Medium
- **Complexity**: Moderate
- **Description**: Add Cross-Dissolve, Wipe, and Slide transitions between video clips.

### 2. Shared Asset Library
- **Value**: Medium
- **Complexity**: Moderate
- **Description**: A cloud-synced library for a brand's logos, transition sound effects, and previous b-roll.

### 3. Multi-Ratio Export (Batch)
- **Value**: High
- **Complexity**: High
- **Description**: Allow exporting 16:9, 9:16, and 1:1 versions of the same edit in a single batch.
